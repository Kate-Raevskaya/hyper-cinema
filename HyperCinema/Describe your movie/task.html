Description

In this stage, we will deal with movie descriptions: title, rating, genres, etc. So, decide which movies/cartoons/series you want to show in your online cinema.

We chose Shrek, a cult cartoon from the beginning of the 2000s, and decided to take all the information about it from IMDB. We decided to use the cartoon Big Buck Bunny as a test video.

What will you choose?

Objectives
  1. Let's bring life to our player and add a link to a video file to it (you can use a local file from your computer or use, for example, the link to the cartoon from Wikipedia). Do not forget that your player should display the controls;
  2. Take a look at your next Figma file. Don't forget the class, id, and tag hints from the previous step;
  3. Choose the movie you want to add to your cinema (or use Shrek). There are conditions for displaying a movie: the title should fit in one line to fit the age rating, the description should be no more than four lines, directors and genres should also fit in one line;
  4. Add all the elements to your page exactly as indicated in Figma. Fonts, sizes, colors â€” everything;
  5. You can use a table to display directors, genres, and ratings;
  6. When you go over buttons, they should change their background and font colors as indicated on the layout (imagine that the mouse is over the Trailer button);
  7. Add a link with the movie cartoon to the player. Lean back and relax.
